Workers’ compensation law can be complicated.

Unfortunately, insurance companies and employers often try to minimize or avoid their liability.  In these situations, it is important to talk with an experienced workers’ compensation attorney to ensure that you are receiving all the benefits that you are entitled to.

If you are hurt at work, you may be entitled to:

  • Medical Treatment
  • Lost Wages
  • Mileage Reimbursement for travel to doctors’ appointments
  • Compensation for Permanent Impairment
  • Other Valuable Benefits, including attendant care, vocational rehabilitation, prosthetics, and housing modificationsdepending on the severity of your injury.
