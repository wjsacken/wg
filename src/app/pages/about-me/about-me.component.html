Wallace & Graham Gives

Wallace & Graham is comprised of over 60 attorneys and legal professionals rooted in three office buildings in the heart of downtown Salisbury in Rowan County. Since our inception in 1981, Wallace & Graham has been focused on charitable giving, particularly in the local Rowan County community.

On April 24, 2020, Wallace & Graham, P.A. proudly launched a charitable giving effort, Wallace & Graham Gives, with an initial $10,000.00 donation to the Rowan County United Way COVID-19 Relief Fund. W&G Gives was launched to publicly show the firm's annual commitment to charitable giving and to allow our firm's employees to have an active stake and engagement in the process.

W&G Gives Mission Statement: To explore issues impacting our community and to develop a responsive, meaningful giving program each year to promote enduring, sincere relationships between Wallace & Graham and the community at large.

The W&G Gives Committee is comprised of current Wallace & Graham employees, who decide annually how to distribute designated funds to the community and/or to our deserving clients. Our board members for the 2022-2024 term are:
